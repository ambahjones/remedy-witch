Policies

Intake/Consent

Appropriate consultation forms will be emailed to you 48 hours prior to your appointment and can be filled out prior to arriving. Forms will be available upon arrival if needed. The session will not begin until all forms have been completed and returned

Scheduled Time

The time for your session has been reserved specifically for you, please arrive 15 minutes prior to you session to complete or return forms. If you are running late your session time may be shortened but full payment is expected for the session

Cancellation/Rescheduling

Please give at least 24 hours notice when cancelling or rescheduling an appointment to avoid fees. If an appointment is missed with no notice, a $30 no-show fee must be paid prior to rebooking
