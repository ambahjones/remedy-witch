Find the Remedy Witch at The Remedy Studio

The Remedy Studio is a holistic wellness center located in Covington Township, Pennsylvania. Opened in November of 2022, Carly couldn't wait to cultivate a space that offered a relaxing escape for the community. It has always been a dream of hers to have her own storefront location with room to grow. Her past experience at immersive spas and wellness centers has influenced the ambiance she strives to create, but on a much more down to earth level. The studio is inspired by the beautiful outdoors and the powerful effects of grounding.
